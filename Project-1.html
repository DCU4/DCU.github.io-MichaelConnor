Neural Networks for Wind Power Forecasting

4th Year - Individual

Assignment: Predict the wind power generation for Ireland

Using pre-supplied training data from Eirgrid, I was assigned to predict the wind power generation across the entire island of Ireland. I used MATLAB to implement different versions of neural networks and tested their performance (Mean Squared Error, training time, complexity). The first revision used a single neuron ADLINE system, which showed poor generalization.
Following on, a multi-neuron MLP network was devised using the Neural Net Toolbox. I ran through various training parameters, including Bayesian regularization and adaptive step size to minimise the output error.
